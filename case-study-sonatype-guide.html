Overview

Sonatype Guide helps teams automate secure dependency choices and policy adoption. The product reduces decision time from days to hours while improving trust via transparent evidence and easy rollback.

Problem & Goals

  • Developers struggle to operationalize component policies inside everyday tools.
  • Security teams need explainable automation that earns trust.
  • Business goal: accelerate policy adoption and reduce mean time to remediation.
Before: policy guidance surfaced late; engineers guessed at fixes.

Research & Insights

  • Field studies with 18 engineers across 5 orgs (IDE + PR review sessions).
  • Preference for inline guidance with a path to depth (progressive disclosure).
  • Automation must be revertible and human‑overridable.

Design System & Key Flows

Wireframes for onboarding, repo scan, recommendation review, and policy change approval. Each recommendation includes confidence and rationale.

First‑run setup reaches a trustworthy recommendation under 5 minutes.
Recommendation detail with confidence + rationale and one‑click apply.
Reversible by default with clear audit trail.
Demo clip (60–90s) walking first‑run → scan → recommendation → approve → rollback.

Impact

  • Decision time for dependency upgrades reduced from days to hours.
  • Increased trust via transparent evidence and rollback.
  • Shipped in two milestones using a modular design spec.
