Designing in and for the Agentic Era

Adopting an ai-assisted design process has allowed me to generate exponentially more value as an individual contributor, both in output and insight. I'm producing smarter designs, faster, while expanding my understanding of how users interact with these tools.

As organizations and users across all industries adjust their processes to benefit from AI, the umbrella of 'product design' expands to cover these agentic experiences. It's pivotal as designers that we can understand these new use cases, design for them, and adapt our products to meet the evolving needs of users.
