About Me

Creating experiences that matter

I've always believed that great experiences, whether in fashion, events, or software, come from understanding what people truly need and delivering it with care.

My path has been anything but linear. I've helped people find the perfect fit as a personal stylist, coordinated unforgettable experiences as an event planner, and even brought stories to life as a voice actor. Along the way, I've been a swim instructor teaching kids confidence in the water, a photographer capturing moments that matter, and a DJ reading the room to keep energy flowing. Each of these experiences taught me something essential: the best outcomes come from paying attention, adapting quickly, and caring deeply about the people you're serving.

What ties it all together is a fundamental belief that users deserve better. Whether I'm designing a garment alteration service, planning a corporate event, or building a developer platform, I'm always asking the same questions. What does this person actually need? What's getting in their way? How can I make this smoother, clearer, more intuitive? This curiosity led me to product design, where I found a home for my systems thinking, empathy, and love of solving complex problems.

Today, I focus on developer experience and platform design. These are spaces where clarity, trust, and speed are non-negotiable. I bring the same attention to craft that I learned styling clients, the same orchestration skills from event planning, and the same adaptability from every role I've held. I don't just design interfaces. I design the experience of getting work done, making tools that feel like they're on your side.
